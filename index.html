School trips come alive through Yello's interactivity!

Scan barcodes found on activities to open up immersive content within your Yello app! Explore media and news for that exhibit, or maybe put some headphones in and enjoy a tour guide.

Collaborate together in groups

Placed in groups, students can compete in challenges against other groups on the trip. Group chats allow sharing of photos and encourage discussion.

Teachers have the ability to join in every group chat and allowing students to easily ask questions whenever!

Learning doesn't have to be boring

Through Challenges, students are set tasks by Teachers within each activity. Each Challenge is worth points which go towards the end of trip leaderboards.

Whether it be to take photos of 10 different spiders, this encourages the students to fully immerse themselves into that activity through exploration!

Never get lost and never fashionably late

Teachers can create itineraries for each school trip. This means every student can understand the timings of the day.

No more shouting teachers reminding students of when to rush back to the meet point! With real time journey tracking and progress bars for active activities lets students know how long each activity remains.
