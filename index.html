Yello

Engage and enjoy every school trip.

Yello is educational app for students and teachers to help them organise and interact with high school trips.

Engage

Interactive expeditions

Scan exhibit barcodes when on an activity to interactively engage with it. Imagine spinning around that T-Rex in full 360º!

Tour

Tour guides without fuss

Have access to audio tour guides with a click of a button! Combine your tour guide as part of Yello's activity scanning.

Organise

Never miss a thing

Never lose track of time or place. View your school trip's timeline, all set up by the teacher – making it easy to manage your time. Never miss out on an exhibit again!

Banter

Have fun, together

Getting split into groups allow for immersive conversations and collaboration of banter!

Challenge

Accept the challenge

Teachers set up challenges for each activity, will you accept the challenge? Reap the rewards as teachers track your progress.
